Resume Builder

Build, edit, and tailor professional resumes with ease. Upload your existing PDF and get started in seconds.

Features

📄

PDF Auto-Parse

Upload your existing resume PDF and have it automatically parsed into an editable format with style preservation.

✎

Live Editor

Edit your resume content with a live side-by-side preview. See changes instantly as you type.

📊

ATS Match Scoring

Score your resume against job descriptions. Get keyword analysis and improvement suggestions powered by AI.

🗃

Version History

Save multiple versions with keywords. Easily compare and restore any past version of your resume.

🎨

Custom Styling

Adjust fonts, sizes, margins, line height, and accent colors. Your resume style is extracted from your PDF.

🔒

Isolated Workspaces

Each user gets their own workspace. Your resume data, versions, and settings are completely private.

How It Works

1

Create an Account

Sign up with your name, email, and password. Takes just a few seconds.

2

Upload Your Resume

Upload an existing PDF and our parser will extract content, sections, and styling automatically.

3

Review & Edit

Fine-tune the parsed content in the YAML editor with live preview. Customize section names and header info.

4

Download & Apply

Export as a professionally formatted PDF. Use JD Match to tailor your resume for specific job postings.
