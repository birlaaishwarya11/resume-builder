# Cover Letter Rules

## Format
- 4 short paragraphs, 1 page max
- Address: "Dear Hiring Manager," (unless name provided)
- Professional but personal tone

## Content
- Don't restate resume bullets
- Use specific stories from the cover letter database
- Keywords from the JD, used naturally
- Show why THIS company, not generic praise
- End with availability and warm close

## Avoid
- Corporate cliches ("passionate about leveraging")
- Throat-clearing openings ("I'm writing to express my interest")
- Em dashes (use commas, colons, semicolons)
- Mentioning visa status or work authorization
- Generic praise that could apply to any company
